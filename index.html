About me

 

Hello my name is Frederique van Asperen a fourth year student from The Netherlands, in my last year I will be focussing on developing my own brand. I want to create different/new content. Like children books, Produce video's and continue developing online escape rooms. Let me tell you a bit more about my books. What makes a good book? Me personally I have never liked reading books. It was a obligation rather than something fun to do. I have actually just started reading for fun this year. Before I haven't touched a book for years, I have always prefered visuals more. But when creating this childrens book I had so much fun that I am thinking about writing another one this time for an older age group.
