Design Justification

I started out drawing the pages I wanted and showing them to friends and family, after adjusting the drawings. I started creating a styleguide according to the feedback received from friends and family. Then the development started. there where some small hickups in the visualising of all content. I decided to create one page with everyone in it. This way it is easy to use, you can easily go through the page with the navigation. This way it is also visually attractive. I also changed some of the colors along the way for asthetic reasons. The buttons all change colors once pressing on them which makes it more interactive for the viewer. The website is created to be viewed on different size screens. I have adjusted a lot along the way to make the website easier to use and more interactive, fun and estetically pleasing. I have worked hard to make the contact form work and redirect it to my thank you page, to give that personal feeling.
